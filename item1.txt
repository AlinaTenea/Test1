Regression:
When are added new functionalities to the app, the testers need to re-execute the tests, to verify that the initial state of the app is unchanged (all features).
Retesting (Confirmation testing)
1) The testers identify a bug - in a feature
2) The bug is sent to developers
3) The bug is fixed
4) The testers re-execute the tests to see if the bug was fixed, is verified only the beahaviour of the bug mentioned. (this is retesting)
